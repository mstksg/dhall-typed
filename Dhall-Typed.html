toTypedKind :: TermCtx us vs -> Expr () X -> Maybe DKind Source #

Convert an untyped Dhall expression representing a kind into a DKind.

Will fail if:

  • The Dhall expression does not represent a kind
  • Any kind variables are involved. Kind variables are not yet supported! But there is no fundamental reason why they wouldn't be; they just have not been implemented yet.

Will behave unpredictably if the Dhall expression does not typecheck within Dhall itself.

toSomeTerm :: TermCtx us vs -> Expr () X -> Maybe (SomeTerm us vs) Source #

Convert an untyped Dhall expression representing a term into a typed one, also determining the type in the process.

Will fail if:

  • The Dhall expression does not represent a term
  • Any kind variables are involved. Kind variables are not yet supported! But there is no fundamental reason why they wouldn't be; they just have not been implemented yet.

Will behave unpredictably if the Dhall expression does not typecheck.
